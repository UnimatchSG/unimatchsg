About

UnimatchSG is a non-profit, ground-up initiative by a couple who first met in university. Having experienced how meaningful it can be to meet the right person during this stage of life, they wanted to pay it forward. Concerned about Singapore's declining birth rate and the fact that finding a compatible partner has quietly become harder in today's fast-paced world, they built UnimatchSG to help undergraduate students in Singapore who are single and open to a relationship meet someone compatible through an AI-driven matching process.

FAQ

Fair questions.

Is this really free?

Yes. No premium tier, no upsell, no ads. We run UnimatchSG on our own time.

Who can sign up?

Single undergraduates currently enrolled at a Singapore university, plus matriculating students starting in the upcoming intake. Aged 18+ and looking for a relationship. Verified via university email.

How does the AI actually work?

It scores compatibility on goals, personality, love language, values, and lifestyle. Hard preferences filter first. Then a mathematically stable matching procedure makes the pairings.

What happens to my data?

Used only for the current cycle, and never used to train AI models. We share your Telegram handle, name, university, year, and a short intro with your match — nothing else. Deleted within four weeks of cycle close.

Why no photos?

Photo-based matching collapses into appearance. We are deliberately not doing that. You will see each other when you meet.

What if I do not get matched?

We're sorry — it does happen. Pools are limited and not every cycle yields a match for everyone. We'll let you know honestly, and we'd love for you to try again next round.

Open to LGBTQ+ students?

Not yet, and we're sorry. UnimatchSG currently matches opposite-gender pairs only. We hope to extend the platform as the pool grows and we have the right support in place. Thank you for bearing with us.

Open to graduate students?

Not yet, and we're sorry. UnimatchSG currently serves undergraduates only. We hope to open to graduate students in a future cycle as the platform grows. Thank you for bearing with us.

When does the next cycle open?

Cycle 01 runs through May 2026. Survey closes 31 May at 12pm. Matches are released 1 June at 12pm. If you miss this cycle, the next one opens in August 2026.
